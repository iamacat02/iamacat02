# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Accept constructive criticism professionally
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders for resolution.
